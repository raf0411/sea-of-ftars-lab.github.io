JUDY

MAGE

In the frost-kissed Kingdom of Ice, there lived an elf named Judy, known for her fiery white hair and compassionate heart. She had been adopted by King Alaric as an infant, her true lineage shrouded in mystery. Growing up, Judy felt a deep yearning to uncover the truth about her birth parents, driven by an insatiable curiosity.

One night, as she wandered through the royal gardens, gazing up at the moon, a surge of energy coursed through Judy's veins. To her astonishment, she realized she possessed an extraordinary power: the ability to control the moon. A mix of excitement and trepidation filled her heart as she realized this newfound gift could be the key to unraveling the mystery of her parents' whereabouts. With a determined spirit, Judy embarked on a perilous journey, seeking answers while wielding her celestial power, hoping that it would lead her to the truth she so desperately sought.
